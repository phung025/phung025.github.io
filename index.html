Research Projects

My research focuses on machine learning, artificial intelligence, advance data structures & algorithms, programming languages, and software engineering. I am currently working on the weight shifting research project with Dr. Arshia Khan.

Weight Shifting

I worked with Dr. Arshia Khan from University of Minnesota Duluth to build a system consists of an iOS mobile application, a Raspberry PI, and a pressure mapping mattress that targets wheelchair-bound patients to help prevent pressure ulcers and study the correlation between the development of decubitus ulcers and sitting duration.
